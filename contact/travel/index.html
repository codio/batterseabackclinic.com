Travel Information

Getting to us by bus

49 High Street Kensington to Clapham Junction
319 Sloane Square to Clapham Junction
345 Peckham-Clapham Junction-South Kensington
239 Clapham Junction-Victoria
19 Battersea Bridge to Islington

Visit the London Buses web site for help planning your journey. Buses stop approximately 70 metres from the Clinic.

Getting to us by tube and train

Sloane Square and South Kensington tube stations are 0.9 miles from the Clinic, approx. 25 minute walk. Clapham Junction overland is a 15 minute bus journey from the Clinic.

Visit the London Underground web site for help planning your journey.

Getting to us by car

Meters are found on Parkgate road and surrounding roads. Alternatively, a large good value car park can be found within Battersea Park (at the end of Parkgate road) approximately 70 metres from the Clinic.
